AS LARGE IS THE DATASET THE MORE ACCURATE WILL BE THE MODEL .


FOR EXAMPLE -- IF WE LEARN FOOTBALL AS MUCH AS WE PRACTICE FOOTBALL WE WILL BECOME MORE PROFESSIONAL.


CATEGORICAL VARIABLES --
1. NOMINAL
2. ORDINAL

NOMINAL ARE THOSE DATA WHICH DO NOT HAVE ANY RELATION BETWEEN THEM.
EXAMPLE -- MALE,FEMALE
GREEN, RED

ORDINAL ARE THOSE DATA WHICH HAVE SOME REALTION BETWEEEN THEM.
EXAMPLE-- GRADUATE , MASTERS , PHD
AS MASTERS IS HIGHER THAN GRADUATE AND PHD IS HIGHER THAN MASTERS.
